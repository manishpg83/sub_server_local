Welcome to the patient diet history portal
The Allermetrix Diet History Portal helps your doctor identify foods that may be causing allergy in a cost-effective scientific approach.

When you click the Allermetrix e-Office link below, you will be prompted to select foods that you eat at least 3 times per week.

Clinical studies indicate the foods you are most likely allergic to are foods you eat often.

After you complete the diet history, your food selections will be analyzed by Allermetrix statistical data software and sent to your doctor. Your doctor will review the data, along with your clinical symptoms, and then choose the appropriate foods to be tested.

When your are ready to select your foods, click on the Allermetrix e-Office button below and you will be prompted to enter the password your doctor has given you. If your physician is not a client of Allermetrix, have them call us at 877-992-4100 to set-up an account.
