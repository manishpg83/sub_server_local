This panel is designed for children 3 years of age and under who exhibit the following symptoms: eczema, (with or without wheezing) chronic otitis, rhinitis, wheezing and asthma (without seasonal exacerbations). Foods will be tested via both specific IgE and IgG4 all other tests performed via specific IgE.

Immunoglobulins: Total IgE

Dust Components (IgE): Cat antigen, Cockroach (German), d. farinae,
d. pteronyssinus, Dog (hair/dander)

 Molds (IgE): Alternaria alternata, Aspergillus fumigatus, Penicillium notatum


Foods (IgE & IgG4): Alpha Lactalbumin, Banana, Beta Lactoglobulin, Casein,
Egg White, Milk, Peanut, Soybean, Wheat, Whey
