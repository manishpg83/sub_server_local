Allermetrix has developed its own liquid allergen method that demonstrates greater sensitivity than solid-phase assays available today. It is calibrated to the WHO (75/502) primary reference standard and reports quantitative specific IgE results as well as modified RAST classes for over 480 different allergens. The liquid allergen used in the Allermetrix assay presents the allergen in a natural conformation rather than denatured on a solid phase. Therefore in some cases the IgE antibodies may see allergen epitopes that are not well preserved on solid-phase tests.
