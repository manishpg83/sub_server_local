This panel has been designed as a comprehensive testing panel to confirm the atopic allergy patient as well as verify the offending allergen(s). The panel includes a total IgE and the following specific IgE's:
Dust components: Cat antigen, Dog hair/dander, d. farinae, d. pteronyssinus, German cockroach

Molds: Alternaria alternata, Aspergillus fumigatus, Cladosporium herbarum, Epiccocum nigrum, Fusarium oxysporum, Helminthosporium halodes, Mucor racemosus, Penicillium notatum, Pullularia pullulans, Rhizopus nigricans

Tree, grass and weed pollens: Pollens included in the panel have been selected based on your botanical region, antigenicity of the allergen and cross reactivity data in order to ensure maximum coverage.
