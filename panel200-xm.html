This panel has been designed as a preliminary approach to confirming the presence of atopy (IgE) in a suspected allergy patient. The panel includes a total IgE and 10 specific IgE's. The specific IgE allergens include the following:

Total IgE
Cat antigen
D. farinae
German cockroach
Alternaria alternata
Peanut
1 regional tree pollen
2 regional grass pollens
2 regional weed pollens
The pollens are selected based on your botanical region, antigenicity of the allergen and cross reactivity data (in order to ensure maximum coverage). Various published studies have demonstrated that this form of test strategy will result in identifying the atopic allergy patient at a rate of 95% or greater.
