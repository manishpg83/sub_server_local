The Allermetrix Allergy e-Office integrates Allermetrix laboratory informatics with your food allergic patient's diet history to recommend test strategies that identify trigger foods. Test results are integrated with our custom elimination/rotation diet through the Allermetrix CLOUD diet application improving food allergy outcomes.


Step#1: The patient goes online (through a link on your website which we provide) and enters their diet history on our HIPPA secured website.


Step#2: The provider can access the diet history on the Allermetrix CLOUD. A diet summary is presented showing the patient's foods in the order of most likely to least likely to react to IgE and IgG4 based on our statistical database. Allermetrix food panels are displayed as well as your custom food panels. If you click on any of these panels, the foods contained in the panel are highlighted and pre-selected in the patient food list. You can additionally select foods you deem necessary to test and print out a pre-filled request form to submit with the patient sera.


Step#3: Patient results will post to the Allermetrix CLOUD within 2 business days of
receiving specimens. Our test results are structured to be directly downloaded into your EMR.


Step#4: Allermetrix provides customizable diet plans via the Allermetrix CLOUD from which you choose foods to exclude or rotate in the diet. The diet software is integrated into our CLOUD test data so the entire process is seamless and there is no charge for the diets. These diets may be modified at anytime to meet the needs of your rotation/elimination therapy
